    *   **Non-Asset Businesses:** The value of fee-based businesses is not included in NAV.
3.  **Strategic Rationale:** A premium valuation requires improved profit growth and stability. Companies could strategically sell assets with large unrealized gains to improve capital efficiency and shareholder returns.
